Sendera

AI-Native Crypto Wallet for the Next Generation of Web3

Sendera is an AI-powered crypto wallet designed to simplify blockchain interactions through intelligent automation, multi-chain support, and verifiable blockchain actions.

Our mission is to make Web3 as easy as having a conversation.

---

Vision

Today's crypto ecosystem is still too complex for mainstream users.

Users must understand:

- Wallets
- Seed phrases
- Networks
- Bridges
- Gas fees
- Smart contracts
- DeFi protocols

before they can fully participate.

Sendera aims to remove these barriers by combining AI assistance with blockchain infrastructure.

Instead of navigating multiple applications, users will simply tell the wallet what they want to do.

Examples:

- "Send 0.1 ETH to this address."
- "Bridge my assets to the cheapest chain."
- "Swap ETH for USDC."
- "Show my portfolio."
- "Track my positions."
- "Participate in prediction markets."
